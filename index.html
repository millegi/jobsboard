You will be someone who, by nature, is curious and looks into details of things. You also demonstrate true ownership which means when you see an issue, regardless of whether or not it falls under your job description, you take the initiative to address it. Issues that you deal with vary in nature and can come from multiple sources, so you are logical and open-minded in your approach and also have be ability to learn quickly.
